How to Use

Enter the quantity of each denomination in the input fields. The total will be calculated automatically. Use the reset button to clear all inputs.

About Cash Calculation

This money counting calculator works by summing the products of each denomination and its quantity. For example, 5 x $100 bills plus 12 x $10 bills would be calculated as: 100 × 5 + 10 × 12 = 620.
